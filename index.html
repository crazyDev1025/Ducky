What is Ducky?

Ducky Token is a community-powered cryptocurrency inspired by the artwork of Matt Furie. Ducky is part of a special collection of tokens that includes other popular characters like Brett, Landwolf, Andy, Pepe, and Birddog. With a total supply of 420,690,000,000, Ducky Token is designed to bring together fans of Furie’s art and create a fun, community-focused digital collectible.

Why Ducky Token?

A Connection to an Artistic Legacy: Ducky is part of Matt Furie’s famous collection. Holding Ducky Token means owning a piece of Furie’s creative universe.

Exclusive Comic and NFT Collectibles: Each comic issue is limited to 100 copies and includes a digital NFT version. This mix of physical and digital collectibles appeals to anyone who loves rare items.

Community Focused: Ducky Token is powered by its community. We’re not just building a token; we’re building a community where people can share, collect, and enjoy the world of Matt Furie together.
